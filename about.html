Our Mission

AtonixCorp was founded with a single driving purpose: to democratize enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure through intelligent automation. We believe that powerful, resilient, and secure infrastructure should not be the exclusive domain of trillion-dollar corporations. Every developer, startup, and enterprise deserves tooling that is transparent, programmable, and built to last.

Vision & Values

The principles that guide every engineering decision we make.

Sovereign

Your data, your infrastructure, your rules. We support private cloud, on-premise, and hybrid deployments with no vendor lock-in.

Scalable

From a single developer spinning up a VM to enterprises managing thousands of nodes – the platform grows without friction.

Intelligent

AI is woven into the infrastructure itself. Predictive scaling, autonomous security, and self-healing remove operational toil.

Open

AtonixCorp is built on open-source foundations – OpenStack, Kubernetes, Calico, Prometheus, and Terraform. No black boxes.

Developer-First

Every API, CLI, and UI is designed with the developer experience in mind. If it takes more than five minutes to start, we've failed.

Reliable

99.99% uptime SLAs are not marketing numbers. We design for failure, test for disaster, and operate for zero-downtime deployments.

Technology Foundation

Production-grade open-source technology, enterprise hardened.

OpenStack

The compute, networking, and storage control plane is powered by OpenStack – the industry-standard open cloud OS used by telcos, governments, and enterprises worldwide.

AMD EPYC 4005 Zen 5

Every node runs on AMD EPYC 4005 series processors with liquid cooling and 3× the memory bandwidth of conventional server hardware, delivering consistent performance under load.

Kubernetes + Calico

Container orchestration via Kubernetes with Calico CNI for zero-trust network policy enforcement across every cluster, namespace, and workload.

NVIDIA GPU Acceleration

NVIDIA A100 and H100 GPU nodes are available across all regions for AI/ML training, inference, HPC simulations, and rendering workloads.

Ceph Distributed Storage

Object, block, and file storage is underpinned by Ceph – delivering 99.999999999% durability with intelligent tiering across Hot, Warm, and Cold storage classes.

Global Anycast CDN

BGP Anycast routing ensures content and API requests are always served from the lowest-latency available node.
